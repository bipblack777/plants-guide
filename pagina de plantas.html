Plants

What is a plant...?
Plants are, by the dictionary; living organism of the kind "plantae".
They grow in a permanent site, absorbing the basics elements, like water and inorganic substances through its roots on the ground. Then synthesizing nutrients in its leaves by photosynthesis using the green pigment called chlorophyll.
Plants are the most important living species around the earth, because it provide us with oxygen, the most important element with the water that let us alive.
There are many kind of plants, like:

  • trees
  • shrubs
  • herbs
  • grasses
  • ferns
  • and mosses.

Why you should have a plant

There's pleny of reasons why you should have a plant by your side. One of them is because plants make us happier, it's amazing what a walk in the park can do, when we get in touch with nature we improve our wellbeing, reduceour mental fatigue, increase our relaxation, and even improve our cognition.
Plants can improve our environment. They can instantly beautify a space, but they can also contribute to improvements we can't see, like indoor air quality. Indoor plants can improve indoor air quality in a few ways. Through photosynthesis, plants absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen, while through transpiration and evapotranspiration, plants release moisture vapor and increase humidity.
Plants can also help and improve our productivity, concentration and creativity while we are working or studiying. here are other sub reasons:

  • Boost moods, productivity, relaxation
  • Reduce stress and fatigue
  • Clean indoor air by absorbing toxins, increasing humidity, and producing oxygen
  • Add life to sterile spaces, offer privacy, and reduce noise levels
  • Be therapeutic to care for

Meat with vegetables

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4 plates
MEAL INGREDIENTS TOOLS
Meat with vegetablesa cup of wine, vinegar, olive oil, 2 onions, 3 (red and yellow) sweet peppers, 2 carrots, 2 garlic cloves, 2 (green and yellow) zucchinies, pinch of salt, sugar and romero.knife, cutting board, a griddle and a pan or wok

Step 1: Prepare the meat for cook, put it on the hot griddle with the cup of wine, the minced garlic, a pinch of salt, sugar and the rosemary sprigs. Let roast till the meat get medium.
Step 2: Wash the vegetables very well and meanwhile the meat is roasting grate the carrots, zucchinies, onions and sweet peppers. Quick-broil the vegetables in the pan with the vinegar and olive oil. A little bit of salt is not bad.
